The Worlds They Inhabit

The setting and societal structure of each novel are fundamental to the protagonist's journey. One is a world of chaotic inequality and vibrant individualism, while the other is a world of absolute control and emotionless stability.

A World of Scarcity & Wonder

Setting: A poor, cramped home overshadowed by a magical, mysterious chocolate factory.

Society: A capitalistic, free-market world with a stark divide between the rich and poor. Individuality, for better or worse, is on full display.

A World of Sameness & Control

Setting: A sterile, seemingly perfect dystopian Community where every aspect of life is regulated.

Society: A controlled, utopian society that has eliminated choice, emotion, and individuality to ensure stability and predictability.
